# Awesome Hivemind Use Cases

## 1) Sales qualification intake
Problem: inbound leads arrive with uneven quality.
Solution: classify leads, enrich with firmographics, route to owner.
Result: triage in under 2 minutes.

## 2) Incident triage command center
Problem: incidents are noisy and cross-team.
Solution: parse alerts, cluster symptoms, assign runbook + owner.
Result: lower MTTA and fewer duplicate escalations.

## 3) Customer support deflection
Problem: repetitive L1 tickets drain ops time.
Solution: route known issues to self-serve workflows with guardrails.
Result: reduced L1 queue volume.

## 4) Compliance evidence collection
Problem: audits require manual screenshots and logs.
Solution: scheduled workflows collect controls evidence automatically.
Result: faster audit cycles.

## 5) Research copilot for product teams
Problem: product discovery takes days.
Solution: launch multi-agent web and feedback research workflows.
Result: decision briefs in hours.

## 6) Partner onboarding checks
Problem: onboarding involves scattered systems.
Solution: run policy, legal, and data validation in one pipeline.
Result: consistent go-live checklist completion.

## 7) Recruiting signal aggregation
Problem: candidate data is fragmented.
Solution: aggregate portfolio, references, and role-fit scoring.
Result: faster shortlist with transparent rationale.

## 8) Procurement anomaly review
Problem: spend anomalies are detected too late.
Solution: scheduled workflows flag outliers and route approvals.
Result: earlier intervention on risky transactions.

## 9) Knowledge base freshness monitor
Problem: stale docs cause operational mistakes.
Solution: monitor content drift and trigger update workflows.
Result: fewer incidents tied to outdated docs.

## 10) Postmortem draft automation
Problem: postmortems are delayed after incidents.
Solution: collect timeline events and draft narrative automatically.
Result: same-day postmortem drafts for reviewers.
